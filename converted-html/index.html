Tippooler

Tippooler is a mobile web app for restaurant managers to more efficiently and accurately accept nightly cashouts and divy up the tippool. Restaurants currently use bug prone excel spreadsheets to capture cashout data, often times leading to an innacurate tippool which short changes either the restaurant, the servers, or the support staff. Tippool is easy to use, keeps data secure and on the cloud, and creates meaningful reports for restaurants and their staff.
Technology: Javascript/jQuery, PHP, HTML5, CSS3 tippooler.com

Repalletize

Repalletize is an interesting, niche product that is revolutionizing the commodity pallet market. There are billions of pallets floating through the US economy every year. These pallets are recycled, reused, and resold, but the market is opaque and disconnected. Repalletize will change all of that by creating a location-based marketplace. Pallet owners will be able to cost effectively recycle pallets and those in need of pallets will be able to quickly and easily find pallets supply. Repalletize launched in more than 50 markets by leveraging existing data sources.
Technology: Google Maps API, Backbone JS, PHP, HTML5, CSS3 repalletize.com
